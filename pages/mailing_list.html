Mailing Lists

The mailing lists, commonly referred to as listserves, are the primary way of communicating in the National Federation of the Blind of Florida and the Florida Statewide Chapter. In the Florida Statewide Chapter, we use two different listserves:

  • Florida Statewide Chapter Listserve — available to members of the chapter. This listserve is moderated.
  • National Federation of the Blind of Florida Internet Mailing List Listserve — available to all NFB of Florida members regardless of chapter. This listserve is unmoderated and open.

How to Subscribe

To join, click one of the links below and enter your name and email in the first two boxes within the following page. Do not enter a password unless you want to set one manually. You will receive your password in clear text monthly by email. Once filled, click Subscribe.

Florida Internet Mailing List

After subscribing, you will receive a confirmation email asking you to verify your request. Follow the instructions and click the link to confirm. Check your junk or spam folder if you don’t see the email. If you don’t receive it within an hour, try again.
Link: Florida Internet Mailing List Subscription Page(link is external and opens in a new tab)

Florida Statewide Chapter Listserve

After you subscribe, you will receive a confirmation email. Click the link in the message to confirm. Your request will then be sent to a moderator for approval. Approval can take up to three days. Once approved, you will receive a confirmation email. Remember to check your junk or spam folder during this process.
Link: Florida Statewide Chapter Listserve Subscription Page(link is external and opens in a new tab)
