Our Meetings

Our meetings occur every third Sunday of each month, accept for major holidays, like easter.

We cover a variety of topics including:

  • Spotlight: We ask members to share something positive that has happened to them recently.
  • Business items: We conduct our monthly business which can include
    • Treasurer's Report
    • Secretary's report
    • committee Reports (if any)
    • Other business relating to the chapter and the National Federation of the Blind of Florida.
  • Announcements: We cover announcements of activities or events that are occuring in our chapter or in the National Federation of the Blind of Florida.
  • Presentations: If we have a guest speaker for a particular month, they will be given 15-20 minutes to present their topic.
  • Advocacy: If there is a particular piece of legislation from the National Federation of the Blind or the National Federation of the Blind of Florida, we will discuss the legislation in great detail.
