Who We Are

The Florida Statewide Chapter is part of the National Federation of the Blind. We provide a network for blind and visually impaired Floridians — and interested sighted residents — who may not have a home chapter.

Anyone from other chapters is also welcome to join. Our focus is on statewide advocacy and supporting blind and visually impaired individuals across Florida.

What We Do

  • Support & Networking: A community where members can meet, ask questions, and form friendships.
  • Events: Access to state and national events, including the annual NFB of Florida State Convention, leadership conferences, and legislative campaigns.
  • State Advocacy: Engaging in statewide legislative efforts that benefit the blind and visually impaired community.
  • Guest Presentations: Meetings feature presentations on topics relevant to the blind experience or that benefit our membership.

Meetings & Membership

When: Zoom meetings every third Sunday of the month at 8 PM (excluding major holidays).

Dues: $10 annually, payable in advance. Funds help members attend seminars and conventions.

Communication: We use a mailing list for all announcements and questions.

We would love for you to join us on one of our monthly calls!
